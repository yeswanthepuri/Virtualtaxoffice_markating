Innovative Tax Solutions

For Every Business

Admin

The Administration module is the foundation of VTO, where you can manage your users and security infrastructure for the entire system. This module is included at no charge and is required in order to use VTO.

Org Chart

The heart of VTO, sharing your organizational structure and physical locations for all users and for use by each functional module. This module is also required to use VTO, and is the core of your subscription.

ExCert

This module allows you to request and store indirect tax exemption certificates from your customers, and issue them to your vendors. ExCert covers all U.S. states, Canada and the PST provinces, many tax types, and the common exemption reasons.

Workflow

Workflow Manager is used for controlling the workflow of common tasks performed by the tax department in a business, separated by business function (based on task lifecycle) using Trackers. The currently available Trackers include Audit Tracker and Notice Tracker, which provide the ability to track all audits and notices received from taxing jurisdictions throughout their lifecycles, and Assignment Tracker, which provides tracking of common activities like tax registrations, voluntary disclosure agreements, and research projects.
